Matilda Sutherland cut her teeth as a DJ with a ‘trial by fire approach’ - that is, no real training only playing in front of real people. Adopting the MTLDA moniker in two-thousand and eighteen, her relationship with DJing has since become heavily centered around the energy put forth by the crowd. Enjoying the push and pull of intimacy between dancer and performer, the way a DJ can hold, lose, and then regain the audience’s captivation at any given time. Sutherland's late night club sets mostly consist of off-kilter psychedelia, hard-hitting rhythms, and a dark conjuring of movement.  

In just three years of DJing, Sutherland has contributed mixes for Melbourne labels Steeplejack and Butter Sessions, maintaining a steady output of mixes and radio appearances. Sutherland has graced the stages of Hugs&Kisses, Lounge and Sub Club countless times, she has supported international artists the likes of Function, SPFDJ and Wata Igarashi - both solo and as part of b2b duo with Nat Salih.

Across 2018 and 2019, Matilda organized Powertrip with Female Wizard. Powertrip is an anti-capitalist endeavour interested in explorative musical experiences, and exposing people to new modes of being with performance and sound. Together they created transcendental sensory experiences in the intimacy of the Gasometer Upstairs, curated an online mix series, and hosted experimental DJing platform Outer Sounds at notorious venue Crazy Arms (RIP).

Girl On Road is the latest project, which Matilda plans to morph into an electronic music record label via her own forthcoming release planned for May of twenty twenty-one. What began as a digital transmission via Skylab Radio has become an opportunity to support artists across the globe who are pushing boundaries in their music, through heavily anarchic undertones and overt politcal statements.

In 2021, Matilda plans to extend herself beyond electronic music, with a forthcoming EP release exploring prior experience as a vocalist and songwriter. Turning over a new musical leaf, Matilda intends to build a rich career as a performing artist and composer on top of what she's started through her DJing moniker, MTLDA.
